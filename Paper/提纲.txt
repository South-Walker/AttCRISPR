Clustered regularly interspaced short palindromic repeats (CRISPR)/CRIPSR-associated  protein 9 (Cas) systems is preferred over other biological research and human medicine technologies now, beacuse of it's efficiency, robustness and programmability. Cas9 nucleases can be directed by short guide RNAs (sgRNAs) to introduce site-specific DNA double-stranded breaks in target，so to enable editing site-specific within the mammalian genome【1-3]. CRISPR/Cas9, to a large extent, has developed genetic therapies at the cellular level, while there are still severe medical disadvantage even now which has greatly hindered the further clinical application of the CRISPR/Cas9 systems. One of these disadvantage is due to point mutations caused by off-target effects. 【4-7】To overcome this disadvantage, a solution is to engineer CRISPR/Cas9 with higher specificities. That's why more and more higher specificities Cas9 variants, such as enhanced SpCas9 (eSpCas9(1.1)) ,Cas9-High Fidelity (SpCas9-HF1)【8-9】, hyper-accurate Cas9 (HypaCas9)【10】, been developed and bring a significant volume of experimental data, that is to say researchers have to face the difficulty of analyzing such huge and heterogeneous data.

Distinctly fluctuant behaviors has been observed for the performance of CRIPR/Cas9 systems with different sgRNAs, some optimum sgRNAs can hit almost all targers alleles, while anothers don't even show activity, 因此开发一个精确的sgRNA活性预测模型是有必要的，实际上，已经开发出了很多用于设计guide RNAs 的应用或工具（找几篇最近综述引），他们或基于传统机器学习方法或基于深度学习模型，一些研究已经表明，当数据集中的sgRNA数量达到一定程度时，deep-learning-based models often 比 conventional machine learning有更好的表现【11】。而基于传统机器学习方法的构建模型往往在可解释性上更有优势。in short,what was needed for developer is to trade-off accuracy and interpretability. BMC那篇认为deep-learning-based models lack interpretability【12】，当然这实际上并不准确，【13】等人使用基于输入扰动的方法在base dimer级别分析了他们deep-learning-based model 中对应的dimer位置的重要性（they use a sliding window of length 2 to extract dimer as input of model），然而并没有精确到具体的碱基类型，而且基于输入扰动的方法往往要求模型拥有更好的泛化能力(甚至要求使用构造荒谬的输入数据，没有证据证明模型对这些人工制造的输入仍具有良好的性能)，进一步的，我们还注意到【14】等人在他们基于RNN的DeepHF模型中使用了Deep SHAP to revealed nucleotide contributions，Deep SHAP is a compositional approximation of SHAP values since it's challenged to computate SHAP values exactly，especially for a complex deep neural networks.【15】而最近的工作【16】指出基于post hoc explanations techniques based on input perturbations的方法，比如SHAP，可能是不可靠，不鲁棒的（用他们的话来说是could be fooled to generate meaningless explanations instead of reflecting the underlying biases）除此之外，DeepHF使用了碱基的生物学特征进行数据增强，which 能够被用来在一定程度上推理出碱基序列信息，在使用生物学特征的网络中运用deep shap无疑会减低可信度，而不使用生物学特征则显然会减低模型的泛化能力，这使得我们有理由怀疑可信度 of 得到的碱基贡献。
